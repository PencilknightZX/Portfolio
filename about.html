About Me

Hi, I'm Emmanuel Ifeanyi, a Computer Science student at California State University, Fullerton (CSUF). A fun fact about me: I was born and raised in Ireland, where I lived for 12 years before moving to sunny California in 2014.

My journey as a developer has been fueled by a mix of technical skills and creative passions. I specialize in programming, web development, and working with REST APIs, FastAPI, React, and Git. These skills enable me to create functional and visually appealing solutions.

When I'm not coding, you’ll find me indulging in my hobbies: making manga and comics, skateboarding, working out, cooking, or immersing myself in movies, shows, and anime. These interests not only reflect my personality but also enhance my creativity and problem-solving abilities as a developer.

I aim to merge my love for technology, design, and storytelling into innovative projects that make an impact. Let's connect and bring ideas to life!
